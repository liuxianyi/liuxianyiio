Dr. Jing Peiguang, received his Ph.D. in Information and Communication Engineering, from Tianjin University. He is currently an associate professor at the School of Electronic and Information Engineering at Tianjin University. He and his group at Tianjin University have published more than 50 refereed journal and conference papers. Dr. LIU has been awarded 10 authorized Chinese patents, with another 5 Chinese patents pending.

Research interest: Multimedia Analysis and Tensor Decomposition. Interested in internship or collaboration? Contact me.
